What OCMT is

Three things we do, for real

01

Education

Certified training in alpinism, mountaineering and canyoning with EMAM, a school recognized by the Federación Madrileña de Montañismo.

02

Community

Born at the IE Outdoors Club, with 1,800+ members bound by the mountains. Fellowship over competition.

03

Stories

Every expedition is documented in photo and video. Micro-documentaries that show the human side of every summit.
