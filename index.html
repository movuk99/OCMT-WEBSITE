OCMT is an international, youth-oriented mountaineering community. We teach alpinism, hiking and climbing to beginners, work as a content hub for athletes and creators, and bring people together worldwide through unforgettable expeditions.

What OCMT is

Three things we do, for real

01

Prioritize Education

We connect future mountaineers with certified guides and advanced schools, so every course builds the skills needed to stay safe in the mountains. Safety over ego, always.

02

Community

We want to unite people who share the same passion and desire for unforgettable experiences. We want to be the pioneers for the next generation of mountaineers around the world.

03

Stories through creation

Every expedition is documented in photo and video. We have a passion for storytelling that show the human side of every summit.
